# Code of Conduct

SanJuan AI is a civic technology project. We want contributors, users, and testers to feel welcome, respected, and safe.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Welcoming newcomers
- Assuming good intent
- Giving clear feedback
- Citing sources when making factual claims
- Being mindful of Puerto Rico's linguistic, cultural, and civic context

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Racist, sexist, xenophobic, or discriminatory language
- Publicly sharing private information
- Deliberately misleading users about official sources
- Abusive or disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, or discussions

## Civic trust standard

Because this project deals with public information, contributors should avoid overstating what SanJuan AI can do. It is not an official government service and should not be represented as one.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you see harmful behavior, contact the maintainer privately or open a minimal issue if private contact is not available. Do not share private personal details publicly.
